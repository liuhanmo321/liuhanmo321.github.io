I am **Hanmo LIU**, a researcher in the Search and Recommendation Lab, **Huawei Singapore Research Center**. I earned my Ph.D. in Data Science and Analytics from the [Division of Emerging Interdisciplinary Areas (EMIA)](https://emia.hkust.edu.hk/) at **HKUST** in 2026, fortunate to be advised by Prof. [CHEN, Lei](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~leichen/) and Prof. [YANG, Can](https://sites.google.com/site/eeyangc/). Previously, I obtained a B.Sc. in Honors Physics and Computer Science from the [Department of Physics](https://physics.hkust.edu.hk/), HKUST, in 2021.

## Research Interests

My research centers on two complementary angles:

- **Model Evolution** — studying how to make models evolve more efficiently and effectively, spanning continual learning on streaming real-world data and self-driven model redesign empowered by large language models.
- **Empirical Science of Large Models** — treating large-model research as an empirical science: starting from phenomena observed on real data, forming and verifying hypotheses, and distilling generalizable principles that guide future research.
